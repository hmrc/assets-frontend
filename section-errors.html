Form Field

The form field in error state is demonstrated by using the .form-field--error selector. Styles are also provide for .error-notification and .error-field to highlight the communication of errors to the user.

Please enter your full name
Please enter your first name
Please enter your surname 
<fieldset>
  <legend>Please enter your full name</legend>
  <div class="form-field form-field--error [modifier class]">
    <span class="error-notification">Please enter your first name</span>
    <label for="firstname">
        Full Name
        <input type="text"
               class="input--medium error-field"
               id="firstname" />
    </label>
  </div>
  <div class="form-field form-field--error [modifier class]">
    <span class="error-notification">Please enter your surname</span>
    <label for="surname">
        Full Name
        <input type="text"
               class="input--medium error-field"
               id="surname" />
    </label>
  </div>
</fieldset>

Form Field Group

The form field group in error state is demonstrated by using the .form-field-group--error selector. Styles are also provide for .error-notification to highlight the communication of errors to the user.

Please enter your full name 
<fieldset class="form-field-group form-field-group--error">
  <legend>Please enter your full name</legend>
  <div class="form-field">
    <label for="firstname">
        First Name
        <span class="error-notification">Please enter your first name</span>
        <input type="text"
               class="form-control"
               id="firstname" />
    </label>
  </div>
  <div class="form-field">
    <label for="surname">
        Surname
        <span class="error-notification">Please enter your surname</span>
        <input type="text"
               class="form-control"
               id="surname" />
    </label>
  </div>
</fieldset>
